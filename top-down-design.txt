Make a Sandwich

1 Gather ingredients
  1.1 Walk to fridge
  1.2 Open fridge
  1.3 Get ingredients from fridge
    1.3.1 Bread
    1.3.2 Sliced turkey
    1.3.3 Sliced cheese
    1.3.4 Mayonnaise
    1.3.5 Mustard
    1.3.6 Banana peppers
    1.3.7 Spinach
    1.3.8 Tomato
  1.4 Place all ingredients on counter
2 Prepare ingredients
  2.1 Prepare tomato
    2.1.1 Pick up knife
    2.1.2 Slice tomato
    2.1.3 Discard stem
  2.2 Prepare bread
    2.2.1 Open bread package
    2.2.2 Remove two slices of bread
    2.2.3 Close bread package
  2.3 Prepare sliced turkey
    2.3.1 Open turkey package
    2.3.2 Remove 3 slices of turkey
    2.3.3 Close turkey package
  2.4 Prepare sliced cheese
    2.4.1 Open cheese package
    2.4.2 Remove 2 slices of cheese
    2.4.3 Close cheese package
  2.5 Prepare spinach
    2.5.1 Open spinach bag
    2.5.2 Remove small handful of spinach from bag
    2.5.3 Rinse off spinach in sink
    2.5.4 Shake spinach to dry
    2.5.5 Roll top of spinach bag to close
    2.5.6 Secure top of bag with clip
  2.6 Prepare banana peppers
    2.6.1 Twist lid of banana pepper jar to the left
    2.6.2 Remove lid
    2.6.3 Remove 5 banana pepper rings from jar
    2.6.4 Place lid on top of jar
    2.6.5 Twist lid to the right to secure
3 Prepare tools
  3.1 Get plate
    3.1.1 Open cabinet
    3.1.2 Pick up plate
    3.1.3 Place plate on counter
    3.1.4 Close cabinet
  3.2 Get knife
    3.2.1 Open drawer
    3.2.2 Pick up knife
    3.2.3 Place knife on counter
    3.2.4 Close drawer
4 Assemble sandwich
  4.1 Place one piece of bread on plate
  4.2 Apply mustard
    4.2.1 Open lid
    4.2.2 Hold bottle upside down
    4.2.3 Position over bread
    4.2.4 Squeeze quickly
    4.2.5 Close lid
    4.2.6 Use knife to spread evenly over bread
  4.3 Apply mayonnaise
    4.3.1 Open lid
    4.3.2 Hold bottle upside down
    4.3.3 Position over bread
    4.3.4 Squeeze quickly
    4.3.5 Close lid
    4.3.6 Use knife to spread evenly over bread
  4.4 Place slices of turkey on top of bread
  4.5 Place slices of cheese on top of bread
  4.6 Place slices of tomato on top of bread
  4.7 Place spinach leaves on top of bread
  4.8 Sprinkle banana pepper rings on top of spinach leaves
  4.9 Place second piece of bread on top
